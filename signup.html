About Munster Waves

Munster Waves is the new surf shop coming to the Munster region and online very soon. We have introduced this weather app for the convenience of all the surfers in the province of Munster. It is our belief that Munster is one of the greatest places on Earth to catch a wave and we can't wait to arm all of you to go out and tackle those monsters.

We're proud of our Munster roots and it is our plan to stock products that reflect this in our shop such as county styled surfboards and wetsuits, as well as apparel related to the individual surfing towns and villages. If you would like to see a particular product when the shop opens, why not drop us a suggestion using the form on this page. Also make sure to check the box to sign up to an email alert once we open so you don't miss our opening sale!
