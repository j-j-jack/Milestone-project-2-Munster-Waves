Doolin Point
Next Beach

The Cliffs of Moher provide a breath taking and intimidating backdrop here. Doolin Point is an exposed reef break that has quite reliable surf and can work at any time of the year.

It's just down the road from the much busier Lahinch so making a trip into the lively town for the social experience is still an option. The ideal swell direction is from the left and there is a lovely right hand reef break here.

With the biggest breaks running along the cliffs this is one for experienced surfers, as it has the potential to get pretty huge. In fact, Ireland's biggest wave, 'Aill na Searrach', is just off the Cliffs of Moher. The wave features in the movie Waveriders.
